the movies.

2020-04-26, by Cagin

How the quarantine is going? I have recently been watching a lot of movies. I started a list at the beginning of this year, I was going to watch at least one movie each week...

I have made it so far, even better than I guessed at that time. Nobody would imagine the time we are living these days anyway. I use Notion to track them all, but I also wanted to have a little space here to add the ones that made me feel somehow different than others.
